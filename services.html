OUR SERVICES
Research Services

Oxstar offers multi-disciplinary research services consisting of proposal development, research design, methodological design, data collection, data analysis, and report development. We also provide research training services and practical online data collection platforms (KOBO-Collect, Survey CTO, and ODK Collect) and data analysis software SPSS, STATA, R and SAS software. Oxstar has a diverse, competent international and local technical team committed to strengthening your organization's capacity to succeed through custom research and analysis.

Needs Assessment

Oxstar offers quality need assessment services before program implementation and design kick-off at the community level. This plays a pertinent role in helping your organization understand the beneficiaries’ needs, perceptions, and interests concerning the program. Our technical team can transcend your organization expectations and efficiency in meeting the donor requirements for humanitarian and development programs.

Market Assessment

Oxstar conducts detailed scientific market assessments for private and public sectors to inform your organization about consumers’ preferences and market demand and supply forces. We also offer network marketing training services.

Third Part Monitoring Process

Oxstar provides ongoing and periodic third-party monitoring services that allow organizations to increase quality assurance of programming and meet standards set by donors and provide beneficiary feedback mechanisms to improve accountability to the stakeholders.

Project Evaluation Services

Oxstar offers diverse project evaluation services ranging from baseline, midterm, and end-line assessments for grass root CBOs, I/NGOs, private enterprises and governmental agencies. Our technical team have expertise in conducting multi-sectoral evaluations such as peace building and reconciliation, livelihoods and food security, agriculture, water, sanitation, and hygiene, health, nutrition, gender based violence, education amongst other sectors. 

Capacity Building & Training Services

Oxstar offers various capacity building and training services, including seminars, workshops facilitation, and institutional-based development packages covering cross-cutting thematic areas. Our professionals in each technical area of business consulting and organizational development work as a team to provide high value-added training services and enhance your organization's growth and prosperity.
