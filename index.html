Historical Background

Oxstar was established in March 2018 during the International Research and Training Workshop held at the Golden Tulip Hotel Dar es Salaam in Tanzania sponsored by the Queen’s University Belfast, Northern Ireland UK to strengthen the capacity of African scholars on crucial research gaps in the continent. Thereafter, the Oxstar Co-founders have been leveraging the capacity of academic institutions, Non-governmental organizations, government agencies and private enterprises to utilize scientific research to solve bottlenecks facing most African countries. Through this humble origin, Oxstar was formalized and legally registered under the Companies Act, 2015 of Kenya (No. PVT-9XUG95VV) as a private limited company that deals in research & development activities in Kenya and internationally. 

Vision Statement

To be the company of choice offering optimal scientific techniques and reliable research & development solutions to generate evidence for decision-making and policy formulation in the dynamic environments in Africa and globally.

Mission Statement

Our core mandate is to provide solutions based on innovation and scientific processes to inform organization’s decision-making and policy formulation in the dynamic African environments through conducting quality research, needs assessment, market assessment, project evaluations, third party monitoring and capacity building & training services to the government agencies, private sectors and development community including humanitarian aid agencies, international I/NGOs, local CBOs, and charity organizations in Africa and globally.

Core Values
  1. Scientific Performance: Demonstrating initiative in supporting the goals and overall innovative strategy of the company.
  2. Team Focused: Developing a culture of high-functioning and supportive teams.
  3. Honorable and Respectful: Contributing to a work environment that honors and respects the needs of each stakeholder.
  4. Sustainably and Socially Conscious: Involvement in practices that increase our positive community impact.
